Ribs

Look at that juicy rib! Goes well with macaroni and mashed potatoes!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups apple juice
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp liquid smoke
  • 2 bay leaves (or more, to taste)
  • 2 racks baby back pork ribs

This will serve up to 8 people!

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients into a multi-functional pressure cooker. Add bay leaves.
  2. Add a trivet to the pot and place ribs on top, trimming as needed. Close and seal the lid; set valve to closed. Set to high pressure for 15 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
  3. Release pressure using the valve for about 15 minutes. Unlock and remove the lid. Done!

Dont forget to check the internal temperature of your pork!
