Brisket

Look at how delicious this is! It pairs well with most BBQ sides!

Ingredients

  • 14 lbs brisket (boneless trimmed)
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp liquid smoke
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 3 tbsp white vinegar
  • 4 tbsp kosher salt
  • 2 tbsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 2 tsp parsley
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1/2 cup cooking wine
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt

This will serve up to 20 people!

Steps

  1. Marinate the brisket
  2. Rub the brisket
  3. Smoke & wet mop the brisket
  4. Let the brisket rest before slicing

Dont forget to check the internal temperature of your brisket!
