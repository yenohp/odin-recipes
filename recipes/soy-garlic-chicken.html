Soy Garlic Chicken

Look at how delicious this is! It pairs well with rice too!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pound chicken thighs (boneless skinless)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp dark soy sauce (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp allspice
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

This will serve up to 6 people!

Steps

  1. Pat the chicken dry and trim if preferred.
  2. Whisk together ingredients (except olive oil)
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and add chicken. Using a brush, brush half of the mixture over the chicken pieces. Cook for 5 minutes, flip and repeat with the remaining sauce. Cook for 7 to 9 minutes, flip over and cook for an additional 2 to 4 minutes. The thighs should take a total of 14 to 18 minutes to be fully cooked.

Dont forget to check the internal temperature of your chicken!
